Dice & Monsters

A toolkit for your D&D table.

⚔️ Encounter Planner Build encounters from 322 SRD monsters, track HP and conditions, roll initiative and run the fight. For the DM. 📜 Character Sheet Create and keep your characters — abilities, skills, combat stats and gear. For the players. 👥 Groups The party at a glance — AC, initiative and passive perception. The numbers a DM needs, without the players' HP. 🗺️ Adventure Run a ready-made one-shot or write your own, then send each scene's encounter straight to the planner.

Everything is stored locally in your browser for now. Cloud sync and group sharing are on the way.
